ASAAL stands for "Alliance of South Asian American Labor". We are a group of workers and laborers who fight for each other's rights in the workplace and in society as a whole. America is a country founded and built by immigrants. People from around the globe have adopted her as their own country. The South Asian people from Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka have been increasingly immigrating to this great country. As an increasing number of South Asians settle in this country, their family members are joining them and leaving their native countries behind. By reaching out to the community of Asian Americans, we can give them a helping hand by providing a voice to under-represented citizens in government.
